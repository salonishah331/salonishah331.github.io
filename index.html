PROJECT ZERO
AUGUST 2019 - DECEMBER 2019

Motivated by the appalling amounts of discarded in Atlanta food banks, our team aims to develop technology to mitigate food waste. Project Zero focuses on restaurant mediums as a source for food waste and examines a possible solution to combat global food waste by first mitigating food waste in these restaurant mediums. By understanding the magnitude of waste consumption restaurants output and their reasons, Project Zero suggests a design of an AI-based inventory management system that would keep track of item count, expiration dates, and other relevant data to maximize the usage of inventory stock, thus, reducing food waste. In addition, the system simultaneous acts as a logistics tool that will automate periodic ordering and will restock the restaurant’s inventory, preventing excessive ordering and other perks mentioned below.

  • Created a two-tier artificial intelligence based inventory management system in order to reduce food waste among restaurants
  • Implemented this system at 10+ restaurants which reduced food waste by over 45%, leading to a 20% overall increase in profit
